## Practice Loop

1. Start with the problem statement and restate requirements out loud.
2. Spend most of your time on the high-level design and failure behavior.
3. Save time for trade-offs, bottlenecks, and scaling assumptions.
4. End with a concise summary that proves you understand the architecture.

## Time Boxing

| Phase | Suggested Time | Outcome |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Requirements | 5 min | Clarify scope and constraints |
| High-level design | 10-15 min | Sketch core architecture |
| Deep dive | 10-15 min | Explain critical components |
| Trade-offs and wrap-up | 5 min | Show judgment and reflection |
